Frequently Asked Questions

What is Edusphere?

Edusphere is PI DOT’s global experiential learning curriculum for students from Grade 5 to 10. It teaches life skills, public speaking, innovation, empathy, AI, global awareness, and more — in a fun, hands-on way.

Is Edusphere a replacement for school education?

No. Edusphere complements school learning by adding essential life and 21st-century skills that regular academics often miss.

How is the Edusphere curriculum taught?

Through workshops, activity books, global facilitator sessions, and immersive learning experiences that are age-appropriate, practical, and emotionally intelligent.

Can Edusphere be implemented in any school?

Yes! We customize and integrate our curriculum into schools across India, adapting to their academic structure and student needs.

How can our school partner with PI DOT?

You can reach out via the Contact Us section. Our team will set up a meeting to understand your needs and design a partnership model tailored to your institution.

Is the curriculum customizable for different boards or student levels?

Absolutely. All content is designed to be adaptable across CBSE, ICSE, State Boards, and international curricula, with grading and structure based on student needs.
