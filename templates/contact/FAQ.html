General PI DOT Questions

What is PI DOT?

PI DOT is a social-impact driven organization focused on transforming education and career readiness. We build experiential curriculums and industry-led training programs to bridge the gap between what is taught in schools and colleges and what the real world demands.

Who can benefit from PI DOT?

Students (Grades 5–10 and college), parents, educational institutions, and companies looking for industry-ready talent can all benefit from PI DOT’s offerings.

What makes PI DOT different from other education platforms?

We combine education, technology, psychology, and empathy to offer globally aware, India-rooted experiential learning that goes beyond the classroom. Our curriculums are practical, skill-driven, and mentorship-led — not exam-focused.

Which PI DOT program is built for schools?

Edusphere is PI DOT’s global experiential learning curriculum for students from Grade 5 to 10. It teaches life skills, public speaking, innovation, empathy, AI, global awareness, and more — in a fun, hands-on way.

Which program connects companies to trained, job-ready talent?

That’s Genesis — our corporate-led education and hiring platform. Genesis trains college students in real-world skills through industry-backed modules and connects them directly to companies looking for pre-trained, role-specific talent.

Why does PI DOT offer multiple programs instead of just one?

Because one-size-fits-none.

Students in school and college face very different challenges, and companies need talent that’s ready on day one. PI DOT is built to bridge these gaps across all levels — from school to career — with focused, purpose-driven programs that create real impact where it’s needed most.

Edusphere Questions

What is Edusphere?

Edusphere is PI DOT’s global experiential learning curriculum for students from Grade 5 to 10. It teaches life skills, public speaking, innovation, empathy, AI, global awareness, and more — in a fun, hands-on way.

Is Edusphere a replacement for school education?

No. Edusphere complements school learning by adding essential life and 21st-century skills that regular academics often miss.

How is the Edusphere curriculum taught?

Through workshops, activity books, global facilitator sessions, and immersive learning experiences that are age-appropriate, practical, and emotionally intelligent.

Can Edusphere be implemented in any school?

Yes! We customize and integrate our curriculum into schools across India, adapting to their academic structure and student needs.

How can our school partner with PI DOT?

You can reach out via the Contact Us section. Our team will set up a meeting to understand your needs and design a partnership model tailored to your institution.

Is the curriculum customizable for different boards or student levels?

Absolutely. All content is designed to be adaptable across CBSE, ICSE, State Boards, and international curricula, with grading and structure based on student needs.

Genesis Questions

What is Genesis?

Genesis is a corporate-led education and placement platform for college students. It trains them in sales, HR, marketing, product management, and more — while connecting them directly to job opportunities.

How is Genesis different from regular college training programs?

Genesis is led by real industry experts and companies. Students work on live cases, build portfolios, and get mentored — not just taught theory.

Do students get job offers through Genesis?

Yes. Genesis is designed to prepare students for specific roles and connect them to hiring companies that need job-ready talent.

How can our college partner with PI DOT?

Through Genesis, we provide companies with pre-trained, role-ready talent. Companies can also co-create training programs or host sessions as mentors.

Is the curriculum customizable for different boards or student levels?

Yes. Genesis adapts its training modules based on the student’s year of study and career goals. First-year students receive foundational exposure, while final-year students undergo advanced, role-specific training. This ensures every student gets the right skills at the right stage of their journey.

How do companies collaborate with PI DOT?

Companies partner with Genesis to access job-ready, pre-trained talent. They can co-design role-specific training, mentor students, offer internships, or hire directly from our talent pool — saving time, reducing hiring costs, and improving employee performance from day one.

Can we hire directly from your student pool?

Yes. We offer a streamlined process to source, train, and onboard talent specific to your industry requirements.
